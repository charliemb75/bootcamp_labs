**Source:** Telegram Trigger
**Destination:** Airtable

**Real-world scenario:**
Someone's birthday is soon and they are throwing a party with some snacks and karaoke. They are allowed to bring more people if they want.
The host wants to keep track of the diet restrictions of the hosts and of the songs they would like to perform.
They ask the guests to send them a sort message with the following fields, which they then send to a Telegram bot, which is connected to Airtable through n8n.
- Guest
- Diet
- Song
- Companions
This way, no information is lost and the host automatically has a clean overview of the guests and their preferences.
